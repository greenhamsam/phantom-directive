Our process

Every project is different,
but we find that most projects roughly look like this:

1. Research

Qualitative and quantitative research to define what your customers most pressing problems are, and their most important user goals.

2. Proposition Design

Defining a customer value proposition, including the business case, potential market size and revenue model.

3. Validation

Rapidly and cheaply prototyping the product (through brochures, websites, or real clickable app prototypes) and testing it with the core market segment to make sure that the product resonates with customers and solves their problems.

4. Product Development

Designing and building the product itself. We can lead the UX and UI design and act as product managers, either by working with your internal development team or with one of our local preferred development partners.

5. Go to Market

Defining the key messaging and marketing strategy, launching the product, and defining and analysing key metrics to observe its success and guide the backlog for future iterations.
